How they are

Kelso is a suburban area which tightly borders multiple villages such as Lexington, as well as the significantly larger urban city of Longview. Kelso was incorporated in 1889, and is the sister city of Kelso, Scotland. Scottish culture is important to many Kelso, Washington residents.
