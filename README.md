# portfolio
## Overview

This portfolio is built with Angular to represent my experience in software and product development.

## Martket Research

### Competitors

Structure: easily navigable, lean, typically single-column w/ cards
Styling: stylish, animated, 1-2 accent colors, uniform
Content: mixed static & dynamic, focused, mostly personal projects
### Audience

Prospective partners and employers typically spend little time on portfolio sites. As with other consumers, aim to convey information as quickly and clearly as possible. 

Do not oversell achievements. Make the work speak for itself.

## Competitive Analysis

### Takeaways

My experience is broad and professional, though less specialized in popular niches of software development.

### Strengths

+ Academic experience in full-stack web development, education, and visual design.
+ 3.5 years experience in board game print production and game product prototyping.
+ Programming CNC machines, conducting performance data analysis.
+ Myriad professional experiences requiring documentation and asynchronous communication.
+ Multiple years experience in project management in customer service and product development.

### Weaknesses

- Most completed work is not available for demonstration due to IP restrictions and NDAs.
- Reluctance to use AI to produce portfolio content.
- Competitors are more specialized in scripting, architecture, or graphic design.
